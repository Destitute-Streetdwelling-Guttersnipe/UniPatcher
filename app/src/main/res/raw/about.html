UniPatcher is a ROM patcher that supports IPS, UPS, BPS, PPF, DPS and XDelta3 patch types.

Additional features:

  • Fix checksum in Sega Mega Drive ROMs
  • Add/Delete SMC header in Super Nintendo ROMs
