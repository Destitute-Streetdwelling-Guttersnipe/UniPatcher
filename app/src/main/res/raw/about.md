UniPatcher is a ROM patcher that supports IPS, UPS, BPS, APS (GBA), APS (N64), PPF, DPS, EBP and XDelta3 patch types.

Additional features:

- Fix checksum in Sega Mega Drive ROMs
- Add/Delete SMC header in Super Nintendo ROMs
